# Unit 01: Describing people's appearance

### Từ vựng

`hói, trọc đầu:` bald

`râu:` beard

`ria mép:` moustache

`kính mắt:` glasses, eyeglass

`xinh đẹp (nữ):` pretty

`trung niên (adj):` middle-aged

`thiếu niên (n):` teenager

`cao - thấp:` tall - short

`mảnh mai:` slim

`If you have` **blonde hair**^[tóc vàng], `your hair is a pale brown or yellow colour, and if you have` **dark hair**^[tóc sẫm màu], `your hair is brown or black.`

`If you have` **curly hair**^[tóc xoăn], `your hair forms^[tạo thành] lots of little curves^[đường cong], and if you have` **straight hair**^[tóc thẳng], `your hair has no curves or bends.`

`When a person` **goes grey**^[grey = gray (US); bắt đầu bị bạc tóc (go + tính từ = diễn tả sự thay đổi trạng thái)], `their hair starts to turn grey because they are getting older.`

`If you` **wear glasses**^[đeo kính], `you always or often have a pair of glasses on your face.`

`If you have` **dark skin**^[da ngăm], `your skin is brown, and if you have` **pale skin**^[da trắng], `your skin is light.`

`If you` **look nice**^[trông ưa nhìn], `your face makes you seem nice.`

### Describing people's age

`Anh ấy khoảng 30 tuổi:` He's around 30. `hoặc` He's about 30.

`Cô ấy ở độ tuổi 20:` She's in her twenties.

### Asking about someone's appearace

`Cô ấy trông như thế nào? →` What does she look like?

`Anh ấy tóc màu gì? →` What colour's his hair?

### Exercise 1

`He's an international basketball player. → Clarlos` is very tall.

`She's a model and she works at all the international fashion shows. → Camilla` is very pretty.

`He wears a hat because he has no hair. → Brain` is bald.

`She will be 14 next week. → Nadia` is teenager.

`He doesn't eat things like chocolate and cakes so he doesn't get fat. → Adam` is slim.

`She's nearly 50. → Layla` is middle-aged.

### Exercise 2

`He **grew**^[nuôi] a` beard `and a` moustache `to make himself look older.`

`She needs` glasses^[eyeglass] `for reading.`

`She had to stand on a chair to reach the shelf^[cái kệ] because she's so` short.

`All the girls like him because he's very` attractive.

`She doesn't eat cakes and chocolate because she likes to be` slim.

`The **main character**^[nhân vật chính] is a` middle-aged `(in his fifties), married accountant^[nhân viên kế toán] from Chicago.`

### Exercise 3

`Tôi nghĩ (I would say → cách nói lịch sự, mang tính suy đoán) có lẽ cô ấy đang ở độ tuổi ngoài 40. →` I would say she's probably in her forties.

`Anh ấy mới 30 tuổi nhưng đã bị hói. →` He's only thirty, but he's already bald.

`Hầu hết bạn bè của anh ấy đều khoảng 30 tuổi. →` Most of his friends are around 30.

`Tôi đã nghĩ anh ấy là người mẫu vì anh ấy rất cuốn hút. →` I thought he was a model because he was so attractive.

`Có một nhóm thiếu niên ở bên ngoài rạp chiếu phim. →` There was a group of teenagers outside the cinema.

### Exercise 4

`How tall is he? → She's quite short, actually.`

`What colour is his hair? → It's blonde.`

`How old is she? → She's probably in her thirties.`

`What's her hair like? → It's dark and curly.`

`Is your sister pale, like you? → No, she has dark skin.`

`What does her boyfriend look like? → He has blonde hair and he wears glasses.`

### Exercise 6

She's a teenager. She has long blonde hair and pale skin. She's very attractive.
