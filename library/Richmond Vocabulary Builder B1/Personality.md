People who like to say and hear funny things have **a good sense of humor**^[khiếu hài hước]. If you enjoy being with someone like this, you can say that they are **good fun**^[vui tính]. If you make a joke, you say something to make people laugh.

If people are happy to do things, you can say that they are **willing to**^[be willing to + V: sẵn lòng, tự nguyện làm gì] do them.

If someone has a lot of energy, you can say that they are full of energy.

## Describing people's personality

To ask about someone's personality, you can say: What's he like?

To describe someone's personality, you can say: He's quite a friendly person.

To talk about something bad in someone's personality, you can say: She can be a bit rude.

He's always… is used for describing what someone often does, often something bad: He's always late!

He never… is used for **complaining**^[phà nàn] about things that people do not do: He never listens.

`He plays baseball for his college team, and swims several times a week. →` sporty

`She finds it very difficult to talk to people she doesn't know very well. →` shy
